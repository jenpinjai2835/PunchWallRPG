Punch Wall RPG Prototype

File:
PunchWallRPGPrototype.rbxlx

How to test:
1. Open PunchWallRPGPrototype.rbxlx in Roblox Studio.
2. You should see a preview layout in edit mode.
3. Press Play.
4. Click training stations to gain Power, BreakSpeed, and CritChance.
5. Click walls to damage and break them.
6. Use coins to buy fist upgrades.
7. Hatch pets at the Pet Egg machine for bonus multipliers.
8. Push Wall Level high enough to fight the Titan Server Wall.

Included systems:
- Runtime-generated prototype map
- Training stations
- Progressive wall levels
- Wall HP/reward scaling
- Fist shop upgrades
- Pet egg hatching
- Crits, luck, break speed, mastery-style progression
- Rebirth shrine
- Basic player HUD and toast notifications

Note:
The file includes a static preview map for editing. When Play starts,
ServerScriptService/PunchWallBootstrap rebuilds it into the interactive version.
